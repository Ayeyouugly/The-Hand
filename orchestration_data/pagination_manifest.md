# Pagination Manifest: "How AI Thinks"

*   **Board Number:** Board 1 of 7
*   **Narrative Phase:** The Hook & Setup
*   **Conceptual Goal:** Shatter the anthropomorphic illusion ("AI reads like a human") and establish the premise that we are looking at the mechanical reality.
*   **Script Segment:**
    "Most people think AI reads your question like a human does. It does not. And once you see the actual mechanism, you cannot unsee it.
    
    Here is what is actually happening inside a large language model in 2026."

*   **Board Number:** Board 2 of 7
*   **Narrative Phase:** Data Ingestion (Tokenization & Embeddings)
*   **Conceptual Goal:** Explain the foundational concept of high-dimensional vectors and how meaning is purely mathematical/spatial relative to other data.
*   **Script Segment:**
    "Your input, whether it is text, code, or an image, gets converted into numbers. Every word or token gets mapped to a high-dimensional vector, sometimes 4,000 numbers deep. That vector encodes not just the word, but its meaning in relation to every other word in the model's training data."

*   **Board Number:** Board 3 of 7
*   **Narrative Phase:** The Core Engine (Attention Mechanism)
*   **Conceptual Goal:** Unpack a very dense concept (scaled dot-product attention). This board isolates the non-linear, simultaneous mapping of token relationships so the viewer's working memory can fully process "the grid of scores."
*   **Script Segment:**
    "Then the attention mechanism kicks in. The model does not read left to right. It runs a mathematical operation called scaled dot-product attention across every token simultaneously. It is asking: which parts of this input are most relevant to each other? It builds a weighted relationship map, a grid of scores, and uses that map to decide what information to amplify and what to suppress."

*   **Board Number:** Board 4 of 7
*   **Narrative Phase:** Architecture & Scale (The Layers)
*   **Conceptual Goal:** Visualize the depth of the model. Shift the concept from a single attention mechanism to a sequential, 96-layer pipeline that acts as progressively complex filtering lenses.
*   **Script Segment:**
    "This happens not once but across dozens of layers, sometimes 96 layers deep in the largest models. Each layer is a different lens. Earlier layers detect syntax and grammar. Middle layers encode factual associations. The deepest layers handle abstract reasoning and context."

*   **Board Number:** Board 5 of 7
*   **Narrative Phase:** The Output Generation (Projection & Softmax)
*   **Conceptual Goal:** Clarify the climactic, deeply technical action: how a "hidden state" becomes a probabilistic guess for the next word. This is heavy enough to warrant an isolated layout to explain matrix projection and softmax probabilities.
*   **Script Segment:**
    "After all 96 layers have processed the signal, you arrive at the final hidden state. That state is projected against a vocabulary matrix containing every possible next token, and a softmax function converts those raw scores into probabilities. The model picks the most likely next token. Then it runs the entire process again for the next word."

*   **Board Number:** Board 6 of 7
*   **Narrative Phase:** The Payoff (Inference vs. Magic)
*   **Conceptual Goal:** Recontextualize all the previous technical mechanics into a philosophical conclusion—that "intelligence" is just high-speed, structured statistical inference built on human patterns.
*   **Script Segment:**
    "This is not thinking. It is inference. Billions of learned weights, tuned to predict statistically likely continuations, given everything it has ever seen.
    
    The output feels intelligent because the training data was written by intelligent people. The model learned to compress and recombine their patterns.
    
    That is the machine. Not magic. A very large, very fast, very structured prediction engine."

*   **Board Number:** Board 7 of 7
*   **Narrative Phase:** Call to Action (Outro)
*   **Conceptual Goal:** Provide a clean, low-cognitive-load sign-off to drive viewer engagement.
*   **Script Segment:**
    "If this helped, drop a like and I'll keep going deeper."

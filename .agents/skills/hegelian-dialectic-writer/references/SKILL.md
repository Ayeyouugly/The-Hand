## Global Script Constraints
1. **AI Visibility:** Prioritize rigid structure, extreme clarity, and direct answers. Do not use conversational fluff. Optimize for search recommendation engines by outlining the debate clearly.
2. **Pure Educational Value:** No CTAs, no "comment down below," and no external links.

## Retention Structure (100% Focus)
You must format the script strictly using this flow:
1. **HOOK (The Thesis/Threat):** Open with a profound quote or a bold, unsettling statement about a current AI reality or looming psychological threat.
2. **SETUP (The Antithesis/Mechanics):** Define the opposing force. Break down the mechanics of the phenomenon and why the debate exists.
3. **VALUE (The Synthesis):** Provide the deep educational value by offering a grounded solution, parallel structure, or a middle-ground realization. 
4. **PAYOFF (Philosophical Resolution):** Leave the viewer with a lingering philosophical thought on how to navigate this new reality.
